  [33X[0;0YThe  method [9XReembeddingsOfSimplicialSphere[109X computes all edge-face equivalent
  simplicial surfaces of the given vertex-faithful simplicial sphere [9Xsurf[109X with
  the  given  genus  [9Xg[109X  if  these simplicial surfaces are orientable or not is
  given   by  [9Xoriented[109X.  Note  that  two  simplicial  surfaces  are  edge-face
  equivalent if the corresponding face graphs are isomorphic (see [14X15.4-2[114X for a
  definition of the face graph). The method [9XReembeddingsOfDigraph[109X computes for
  a  3-connected  cubic  planar  graph  all simplicial surfaces with the given
  genus  [9Xg[109X  and if these simplicial surfaces are orientable or not is given by
  [9Xoriented[109X  that have [9Xdigraph[109X as their face graph. We call this a re-embedding
  of  a  digraph  or  a  simplicial  sphere.  If [9Xsurf[109X is not a vertex-faithful

  It is not checked whether [9Xdigraph[109X is a 3-connected graph.[133X
  
  [33X[0;0YNote  that,  non-orientable  surfaces  of  genus  one are projective planes,
  orientable  surfaces  of  genus  one are tori and non-orientable surfaces of
  genus two are Klein bottles.[133X
  
  [33X[0;0YFor example, consider the complete graph on four vertices:[133X
